About us

WHY AGC IS THE BEST CHOICE FOR THE BRIGHT FUTURE

Our college is a well-established and reputed institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering the overall development students. Since its establishment, the college has been committed to academic excellence, innovation, and the development of professional skills among students. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields such as engineering, management, and computer applications, supported by highly qualified and experienced faculty members. The campus is equipped with modern infrastructure, including well-maintained classrooms, advanced laboratories, a rich library, and excellent sports facilities,creating a perfect environment for learning and growth. Along with academics, the college actively promotes co-curricular and extracurricular activities such as cultural events, technical fests, seminars, and workshops to enhance students’ creativity and confidence. The institution also provides strong placement support by organizing training sessions, internships, and campus recruitment drives with reputed companies. With a focus on discipline, values, and career-oriented education, our college aims to shape responsible, skilled, and successful individuals who can contribute positively to society.
